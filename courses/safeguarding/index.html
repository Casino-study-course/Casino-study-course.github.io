About our Safeguarding courses


Having an understanding of safeguarding is a requirement of anyone working with vulnerable people. Our safeguarding courses fulfil this requirement by teaching you signs and symptoms of abuse and how to work to keep others away from harm.

All of our safeguarding courses are studied online and are fully CPD certified. This means that they have all met the robust requirements set by the CPD Certification Service. At the end of a course you complete a multiple choice quiz which can be attempted as many times as needed to pass. Then simply download your certifiate immediately and wait for your paper copy to be posted the next day.

Unsure where to get started? We offer specific safeguarding courses for people working with children or if you work with vulnerable adults.
