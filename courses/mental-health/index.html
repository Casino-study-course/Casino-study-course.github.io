About our Mental Health courses


Mental health is something everyone should have an understanding of. Our mental health courses help to develop your knowledge of different mental health challenges and are a great way for you to stay current with mental health legislation if your job role requires this.

Our mental health courses are all CPD certified which means that, when completed, you recieve a fully regulated proof of continuous professional development. Take as long as you need on the course and work through the multiple choice quiz as many times as you need in order to pass.

With specific courses in various areas of mental health as well as our mental health awareness course, we can provide courses to anyone needing training in mental health for their career or simply to understand a particular topic more.
