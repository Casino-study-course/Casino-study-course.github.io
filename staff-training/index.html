Quality Staff Training

Efficient staff training for any sized team with a full management system for you to track progress.

Manage staff easily

Add staff to courses immediately, right in your account.

With your account you can add colleagues to courses and they will automatically be sent over their login details. You’ll then be able to keep an eye on their progress and see the percentage of the course they have completed.

To pass, student have to work through all of the lessons and then pass the final quiz. This quiz can be tackled as many times as needed until a pass grade is achieved and each question is multiple choice.

Our customer service support is open 9am-5pm Monday to Friday if you or your staff have any questions or need any help. We pride ourselves on giving outstanding customer service and want to make sure customers have a great experience with us.

Our customers us

Simple staff training

Our courses are easy to navigate and come with next day postage on all certificates.

Once logged in, your colleagues can easily work through their course in 2-3 hours. It is very rare that anyone ever needs help but if they do, our staff are here to assist.

After completion, students can download their certificate immediately and we also send a quality printed version on high quality 170 GSM card the very next day.

Our certificates look awesome and are fully CPD regulated. They can be used to prove training to regulatory bodies, customers and compliance managers.

30 day invoicing

Get 30 day invoicing on large orders.

We realise there may be certain processes required in your organisation to sign off on purchases. For this reason we offer a simple 30 day invoicing option.

If you need to sort a purchase order and send this to us it is no problem. Likewise, feel free to just email details for the invoice and our friendly team will sort the paperwork right away.
